# Phase 4 MVP Commercial Launch

Status: prototype complete as of 2026-05-25.

## Implemented Scope

- Dedicated `/launch` workspace for the MVP commercial launch.
- Pricing v1 represented as structured packages:
  - Starter model: 30-50 EUR per approved model.
  - Standard verified model: 70-120 EUR per approved model.
  - Hosted pages: 49-99 EUR per month.
  - Optional regeneration fee for extra versions.
- Pilot merchant pipeline for 5 furniture/home decor merchants.
- Per-merchant target product counts in the 10-30 product range.
- Case study discount eligibility marker for early public proof.
- Launch metrics for upload speed, approval pass rate, model cost, hosted load speed, AR clicks, CTA clicks, and renewal.
- Billing page updated to reuse the Phase 4 pricing model and show current published page usage.

## Product Rules Preserved

- The MVP still sells hosted public product page links first.
- Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, SDK, iframe embeds, and construction workflows remain deferred.
- Manual model approval remains required before a public hosted link can go live.
- Commercial claims stay at "Verified AR preview" quality, not CAD/manufacturing precision.

## Deferred

- Stripe checkout, subscriptions, tax handling, and payment webhooks.
- Invoice PDF generation and account balance tracking.
- CRM integration for pilot outreach.
- Production cost accounting for each generation provider run.
- Renewal reporting after the first paid month.
